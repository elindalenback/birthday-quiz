The Quiz Rules:

The rules are simple to follow. When you press 'Start Quiz' there will be a question displayed and 4 options to choose for each question. Choose the option you think is the correct one and if it is correct the color will turn green. If it is the wrong answer the color will turn red. When you worked your way through all the questions you will get your score as well as the option to send it to your email. Why don't you forward that email to Amanda and let her know how it went! After this feel free to redo the quiz as many times as you want. Enjoy and best of luck!
